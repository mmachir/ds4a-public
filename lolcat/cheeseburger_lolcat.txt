cheezburger iz hamburger topped wif cheese. Traditionally, teh slice ov cheese iz placed on top ov teh meat patty. teh cheese iz usually addd 2 teh cooking hamburger patty shortly b4 serving, which allowz teh cheese 2 melt. cheezburgers can include variations in strucchur, ingredients an composition. As wif othr hamburgers, cheezburger cud include toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, bacon, mayonnaise, ketchup, an mustard.

In fast food restaurants, teh cheese usd in cheezburgers iz usually processed cheese. othr meltable cheeses cud bees usd as alternativez. Common examplez include cheddar, Swiss, mozzarella, blue Cheese, an pepper jack. 

By teh late 19th century, teh vast grasslands ov teh Great Plains had been opened up 4 cattle ranching. dis made it posible 4 lotz da Americans 2 consume beef almost daily. teh hamburger remainz as wan ov teh cheapest sourcez ov beef in America.[1]

addin cheese 2 hamburgers became popular in teh late-1920s 2 mid-1930s. thar r several competin claimz as 2 hoo creatd teh furst cheezburger. Lionel Sternberger iz reputed 2 has introducd teh cheezburger in 1926 at teh age ov 16. He wuz werkin as fry cook at hims father's Pasadena, California sandwich shop, "The Rite Spot", an "experimentally droppd slab ov American cheese on sizzling hamburger."[2][3][4][5][6] An early example ov teh cheezburger appearing on menu iz 1928 menu 4 teh Los Angeles restaurant O'Dell's which listed cheezburger smothered wif chili 4 25 cents.[7][8][9]

othr restaurants also claim 2 has invented teh cheezburger. 4 example, Kaelin's Restaurant in Louisville, Kentucky, sed it invented teh cheezburger in 1934.[10] wan yer latr, trademark 4 teh naym "cheeseburger" wuz awarded 2 Louis Ballast ov teh Humpty Dumpty Drive-In in denvr, Colorado.[11] accordin 2 Steak 'n Shake archives, teh restaurant's founder, Gus Belt, applied 4 trademark on teh werd in teh 1930s.[12][13][14]

teh steamed cheezburger, variation almost exclusively servd in central Connecticut, iz believed 2 has been invented at restaurant calld Jack's Lunch in Middletown, Connecticut, in teh 1930s.[15]

teh largest cheezburger evr made weighed 2,014 pounds (914 kg). It iz sed 2 has includd "60 pounds (27 kg) ov bacon, 50 pounds (23 kg) ov lettuce, 50 pounds (23 kg) ov sliced onions, 40 pounds (18 kg) ov pickles, an 40 pounds (18 kg) ov cheese." dis record wuz set in 2012 by Minnesota's Black Bear Casino, breaking teh previous record ov 881 pounds (400 kg).[16]
